Years of Experiences

7 years in GS Engineering & Construction as Piping Stress Engineer

Career Summary

Mr. CJ Choi is skilled in structural & thermal finite element analysis(FEA). He has applied his FEA skills to nozzle evaluation of pressure vessel, design of ASME B31.3 process piping, piping components, piping supports and etc based on ASME BPVC Sec VIII-2 Part.5. Also he has performed B31.1 and B31.3 piping flexibility analysis for mostly oil and gas refinery and LNG facilities. Design, selection, technical bid evaluation and field installation review of the expansion joint are his other technical expertise.
